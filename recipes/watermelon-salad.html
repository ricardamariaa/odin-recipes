Watermelon salad

Description

This recipe is perfect for summer. The refrigerated ingredients will keep you cool in the summer sun.

Ingredients

  • A quarter watermelon
  • Cucumber
  • Feta
  • Mint
  • A lemony dressing
  1. Cut the watermelon and cucumber into mouth sized chunks.
  2. Prepare the lemony dressing, using lemon and olive oil, some salt and pepper.
  3. Crumble some feta over the watermelon and cucumber.
  4. Wash some mint leaves and garnish the salad with them.
