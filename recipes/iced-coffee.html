Iced coffee

Description

Iced coffee is the summer version of your beloved cappuccino.

Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • Milk of your choice
  • Ice cubes
  1. Grind the coffee at a rather fine setting (between espresso and pour over). Ca. 15 g of coffee per 200 ml of water is recommended.
  2. Prepare the coffee in a stovetop coffee maker (or an espresso machine, if you have one).
  3. Into a big glass (350 ml), put a lot of ice cubes and pour the milk until three quarters are filled.
  4. Pour the freshly prepared coffee over ice cubes and milk.
